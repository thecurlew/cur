Curlew - A symbol of beauty and resilience

Hi, I am Noah, an 11-year-old boy from Europe. Since I was young, I have been deeply passionate about birds and animals, inspired by my father, a dedicated advocate for flora and fauna. In 2024, I learned from him that the slender-billed curlew was officially declared the first extinct bird. This elegant creature, last seen in Morocco in 1995, remains alive in my heart. To honor its memory, and with my dad’s support, I created a memecoin called "Curl the Curlew." This project is not only a tribute to the curlew but also a call to action to inspire people to protect birds and the environment. I imagine Curl soaring through the skies alongside his friend, the dodo, as symbols of environmental awareness. Together, they remind us of the importance of caring for our planet. With your help, we can ensure that Curl the Curlew not only lives on in our hearts but also inspires real actions to protect nature. Together, we can transform the curlew into a legendary symbol of hope and change.
